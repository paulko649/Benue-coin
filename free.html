FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about Benue Coin

Is Benue Coin a scam or Ponzi scheme?

Absolutely not. Benue Coin is a real cryptocurrency with its own blockchain technology. Unlike Ponzi schemes that pay early investors with money from new investors, Benue Coin mining rewards come from the blockchain itself through the proof-of-work algorithm. All transactions are transparent and verifiable on the blockchain.

Why can't I withdraw until 2028?

The 2028 withdrawal timeline is a strategic decision to ensure the long-term stability and growth of Benue Coin. This allows us to:

  • Build a robust ecosystem of merchants and services that accept BNC
  • Establish fair market value through organic growth
  • Protect early miners from premature market fluctuations
  • Ensure sufficient liquidity when withdrawals begin

Think of this as a long-term investment that will pay off significantly when withdrawals open.

How is Benue Coin different from Bitcoin?

While inspired by Bitcoin, Benue Coin has several key advantages for Africans:

  • Accessible Mining: Optimized for mobile devices and low-power computers
  • African Focus: Designed with African economic conditions in mind
  • Staged Growth: Controlled release prevents early whale domination
  • Community Focus: Rewards are distributed more evenly among participants
  • Burning Mechanism: Unique burn feature allows compounding of rewards

What happens when I burn my coins?

Burning coins removes them permanently from circulation and in return boosts your mining rate. For example, burning 10,000 BNC might give you a 15% permanent increase in your mining speed. This:

  • Increases the value of remaining coins through reduced supply
  • Rewards long-term holders with faster accumulation
  • Creates a sustainable economic model for the network

Burned coins are sent to an unspendable address and can be verified on the blockchain.

How can I be sure this is only for Africans?

Benue Coin implements several measures to ensure it primarily benefits Africans:

  • Geofencing technology restricts mining to African IP addresses
  • KYC verification requires African government-issued ID
  • Withdrawals will only be possible to African bank accounts
  • The core development team is entirely African-based

We're committed to keeping this an African empowerment project.
